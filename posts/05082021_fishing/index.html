Fishing Spring 2021

Posted at — May 8, 2021

Just a small post/update. Fishing season for trout opened up April 3rd and this spring has been pretty good fishing. I’ve mostly been getting stocked fish but that’s OK by me. I’ve been spending most of my time this spring learning tenkara fishing. I picked up a starter rod and have been trying that out. I’m not as “good” at it as western fly fishing but its a ton of fun. It’s sort of like the manual transmission of fishing. The flies are also really cool looking.
